What is Power Armor?

Power Armor is a form of robotic armor also known as an exoskeleton. It is an advanced fusion powered battle armor from the Fallout Universe which provides incredible protection from radiation, smoke and other particulates, air borne diseases, as well as physical and thermal protection. The suit also allows heavy lifting and use of larger weaponry thanks to its advanced servos that assist movement in the heavy armor. The suits were a key tool for the us army in many battles the T-51b proved pivotal in the battle of anchorage. They became even more valuable after the bombs fell as they provide protection the now immense radiation as well as protection from the harsh mutated wildlife.

The suit has seen use from many factions from official us army use, to civilian mining applications. Post war it saw use from the two branches left by the us governments remains (the Enclave and the Brotherhood of Steel) to the advanced Institute who built their own, to even raiders and looters forging scrap armor onto the broken frames left behind from older military power armor.
